Resident Services & Fees

This page gives you a clear overview of all resident services managed by Mirpur DOHS Porishod—covering Security, Maintenance & Beautification, Mosque & Religious Affairs, Health, Hygiene & Sanitation, Sports & Culture, and Administration & Membership. You’ll also find transparent monthly subscriptions for Plot Owners, Flat Owners, Tenants, and Offices (AGM charge is yearly), plus guidance on how to request a service.

Community Care & Resident Services

Security & Access Control

24/7 monitoring, ID checks, visitor passes, and coordinated emergency response for a safe, orderly community.

Maintenance & Beautification

Road, drainage, and lighting upkeep; landscaping, cleaning drives, and greenery to keep the area pristine.

Mosque & Religious Services

Support for Jumu’ah arrangements, special programs, and orderly access managed with respect and discipline.

Health, Hygiene & Sanitation

Waste collection coordination, public-area cleaning, fogging as needed, and liaison on water / hygiene notices.

Sports & Culture

Community leagues, Pohela Boishakh programs, marathons, and youth activities that bring residents together.

Administration & Membership

Membership processing, records & forms, community notices, AGM coordination, and resident support.
