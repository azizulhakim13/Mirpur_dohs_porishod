Who We Are

Mirpur DOHS Porishod serves one of Dhaka’s most prestigious residential communities—planned by the Armed Forces of Bangladesh for retired defense personnel and grown into a model neighborhood of order, discipline, and modern urban living. Within the Mirpur area, our community is defined by well-organized infrastructure: wide roads, modern drainage, clean footpaths, and generous greenery. Continuous security monitoring and clear community guidelines help ensure a peaceful, respectful environment for families and businesses.

Guided by shared values and mutual respect, the Porishod (Welfare Association) collaborates with residents to sustain a safe, connected, and thriving neighborhood. We oversee core services—Security, Maintenance & Beautification, Mosque & Religious Affairs, Health, Hygiene & Sanitation, Sports & Culture, and Administration & Membership—with transparent subscriptions and accountable stewardship. Our focus is simple: dependable services, clear communication, and a high standard of community life today and for future generations.

Our Journey as a Community

From a planned residential enclave to a model neighborhood, Mirpur DOHS Porishod has grown through disciplined management, resident collaboration, and continuous service improvement.

Founding & Planning

Established as a planned residential community with clear rules, orderly plots, and a welfare association framework.

Infrastructure & Security

Development of wide roads, modern drainage, lighting, and access control supported by a dedicated control room.

Resident Services

Structured subscriptions fund core services: Security, Maintenance & Beautification, Mosque & Religious Affairs, Health & Sanitation, Sports & Culture, and Administration.

Digital & Transparency

Clear notices, helpline directory, and downloadable forms keep residents informed and make services easy to access.
