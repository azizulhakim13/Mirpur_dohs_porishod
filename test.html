About Mirpur DOHS Porishod

Mirpur DOHS is one of Dhaka’s most respected and secure residential communities, developed by the Armed Forces of Bangladesh. Originally designed for retired defence personnel, it has grown into a model neighborhood—known for order, discipline, and modern urban living—welcoming families who value safety, greenery, and a peaceful environment.

President’s Message

“Mirpur DOHS was envisioned as a calm, well-ordered neighborhood where families feel secure and respected. That spirit—discipline, courtesy, and service—still guides us today. We work to keep our roads clear, our footpaths clean, and our surroundings green, while ensuring a quiet, dignified environment for all. I invite every resident to stay engaged with notices, follow community guidelines, and take part in initiatives that strengthen our shared home.”

Senior Vice President’s Message

“Good communities run on simple, reliable services—security that is attentive, maintenance that is regular, sanitation that is timely, and programs that bring people together. Our subscriptions are straightforward and transparent, and our control room is here to help whenever you need support. If something needs attention, let us know early; we will respond clearly and keep you updated. Together, we can keep daily life smooth and respectful for everyone.”

Vice President’s Message

“Our focus is the basics done well—sound infrastructure, orderly traffic, dependable utilities, and clean public spaces. We coordinate closely with relevant services so issues are logged, resolved, and prevented where possible. Thank you for observing the community rules and for treating shared areas with care. Your cooperation helps us preserve the quiet, safe character that makes Mirpur DOHS special.”
